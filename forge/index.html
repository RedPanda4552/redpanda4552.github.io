What is Minecraft Forge and What are Forge Mods?

Minecraft Forge is a modding project that allows mods to be made for both the Minecraft server and client. Mods can be made to depend on a client or server; that is, one mod may only be installed on a client and run, but another mod may need to be installed on the client and also the server to work. Often times, Forge servers have a specific mod set, and players must also have this mod set to join.

Mod Releases

DubzHud

My first Forge mod release, DubzHud adds a hud layer composed of what I call modules and notifications. Modules are displayed in a line across the top of the screen, and display information that otherwise is inaccessible or requires the full debug screen to be open. Notifications stack vertically under the left most module, and display more dynamic information such as swing cooldown, food saturation and low armor durability warnings.
